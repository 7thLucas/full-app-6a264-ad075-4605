# Product Overview

## Product
**Name:** Residential Cleaning CRM
**Type:** Customer Relationship Management (CRM) software
**Category:** Vertical SaaS — Field Service / Home Services

## What It Is
A purpose-built CRM designed for residential cleaning businesses of all sizes — from solo operators to multi-crew companies. It centralizes client management, job scheduling, team coordination, invoicing, and customer communication into a single, easy-to-use platform tuned to the specific workflows of house and apartment cleaning services.

## Target Users
- **Business owners & managers** — oversee operations, track revenue, manage clients, and grow the business
- **Office staff / dispatchers** — handle bookings, schedule crews, and communicate with clients
- **Cleaning crews / technicians** — view daily job assignments, access client notes, and confirm job completion

## Core Problems Solved
1. **Scattered client data** — contacts, preferences, and service history spread across spreadsheets, sticky notes, and emails
2. **Scheduling chaos** — double-bookings, missed appointments, and no clear visibility into crew availability
3. **Lost recurring revenue** — clients lapse because follow-ups and re-booking reminders fall through the cracks
4. **Manual, slow billing** — invoices created by hand, late payments, and no clear payment tracking
5. **Poor team coordination** — crews lack easy access to job details, entry instructions, or client preferences

## Core Features
- **Client Management** — centralized profiles with contact info, service history, cleaning preferences, entry details, and notes
- **Job Scheduling & Booking** — calendar-based scheduling for recurring and one-time jobs; drag-and-drop rescheduling
- **Crew Assignment & Dispatch** — assign jobs to crews or individual cleaners; track availability and workload
- **Automated Reminders & Communication** — SMS/email confirmations and reminders to clients; job-ready notifications to crews
- **Invoicing & Payments** — automatic invoice generation, online payment collection, and payment status tracking
- **Service Checklists** — per-job or per-client cleaning checklists to ensure consistent service quality
- **Reporting & Analytics** — revenue tracking, job completion rates, client retention, and crew performance

## Positioning
*The CRM built for the way cleaning businesses actually work* — not a generic tool forced into a new context, but a vertical solution with terminology, workflows, and features tuned to residential cleaning. Competes on focus, ease of use, and industry fit against generic CRMs (HubSpot, Zoho) and broad field-service platforms that aren't cleaning-specific.

## Brand & Tone
Professional yet approachable. Clean, organized, and trustworthy — reflecting the service itself. Language is plain, practical, and direct: no jargon, no fluff. The product should feel like a reliable team member, not enterprise software.

## Strategic Principles
- **Vertical focus over breadth** — every feature decision is filtered through "does this serve a residential cleaning business?"
- **Time-to-value** — operators should be managing their first client within minutes of signing up
- **Mobile-first for crews, desktop-first for owners** — designed for both contexts without compromising either
- **Automation reduces admin burden** — reminders, invoices, and follow-ups run themselves so owners can focus on growth
